### 详细描述

**What it does:**
MeetSum AI uses advanced AI to automatically transcribe your meeting recordings and generate structured, actionable summaries. Save hours of manual note-taking and never miss an important decision or action item again.

**How it works:**
1. Upload your meeting audio or recording
2. AI automatically transcribes the content
3. Get instant structured summaries with:
   - Executive summary
   - Key decisions
   - Action items with assignees
   - Participant list
   - Sentiment analysis

**Key Features:**
- Multi-language transcription support
- 7 AI providers (DeepSeek, OpenAI, Zhipu, Doubao, SiliconFlow, OpenRouter, Ollama)
- Multiple summary templates (bullet points, narrative)
- User authentication and history
- Clean, modern UI

**Why it's better:**
- Cheaper than competitors (starting at $9/month)
- Supports free AI models (DeepSeek, Zhipu)
- Fast transcription with Whisper
- No complex setup required
