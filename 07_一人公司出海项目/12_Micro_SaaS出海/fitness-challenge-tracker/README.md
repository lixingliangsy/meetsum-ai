# 🏋️ Fitness Challenge Tracker

[![Deploy with Vercel](https://vercel.com/button)](https://vercel.com/new/clone?repository-url=https://github.com/yourusername/fitness-challenge-tracker)

> Transform your fitness journey with our challenge tracker. Set goals, monitor progress, and stay motivated.

## ✨ Features

- ✅ **Custom Challenges**: Create 7-day, 30-day, or custom challenges
- ✅ **Progress Tracking**: Log daily workouts and monitor streaks
- ✅ **Social Sharing**: Share your progress with friends for accountability
- ✅ **Automated Reminders**: Never miss a day with smart notifications
- ✅ **Achievement Badges**: Earn rewards for consistency and milestones

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Choose Challenge**: Select a pre-made challenge or create your own
2. **Log Daily**: Record your workouts and track progress
3. **Stay Motivated**: View stats and celebrate milestones
