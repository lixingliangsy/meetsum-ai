# 🏋️ AI Fitness Planner

[![Deploy with Vercel](https://vercel.com/button)](https://vercel.com/new/clone?repository-url=https://github.com/yourusername/ai-fitness-planner)

> Get a custom fitness plan tailored to your goals, schedule, and equipment. AI creates workout and meal plans instantly.

## ✨ Features

- ✅ **AI Plan Generation**: Get personalized workout plans in seconds
- ✅ **Goal-Based**: Optimize for weight loss, muscle gain, or endurance
- ✅ **Equipment Adaptable**: Plans for home, gym, or minimal equipment
- ✅ **Progress Tracking**: Log workouts and adjust plans dynamically
- ✅ **Nutrition guidance**: Receive meal suggestions aligned with your plan

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Set Goals**: Input your fitness objectives and constraints
2. **Generate Plan**: Let AI create your personalized schedule
3. **Track & Adjust**: Log progress and refine your plan
