Q2: How does Meeting Mind's AI transcription and summarization work?

Answer: Meeting Mind uses a multi-model AI pipeline: speech-to-text (99% accuracy in 40+ languages), speaker diarization (identifies who said what), and LLM-based summarization that extracts decisions, action

Read detailed explanation
Meeting Mind uses a multi-model AI pipeline: speech-to-text (99% accuracy in 40+ languages), speaker diarization (identifies who said what), and LLM-based summarization that extracts decisions, action items, and key points. The AI was trained on 100,000+ hours of business meetings and understands domain-specific terminology across 20+ industries. 1. **Audio Capture:** Records meeting audio (browser-based or bot that joins Zoom/Meet/Teams) 2. **Speech-to-Text:** Converts speech to text with 99% accuracy (Word Error Rate: 1.2%) 3. **Speaker Diarization:** Labels speakers (requires 30 seconds of voice samples per speaker) 4. **Summarization:** LLM extracts: Decisions, Action Items, Key Discussion Points, Follow-ups 5. **Post-Processing:** Formatting, bullet points, owner assignment for action items ---
