# 🤖 PromptGen Pro

[![Deploy with Vercel](https://vercel.com/button)](https://vercel.com/new/clone?repository-url=https://github.com/yourusername/promptgen-pro)

> The ultimate AI prompt engineering toolkit for ChatGPT, Midjourney, DALL-E, and more.

## ✨ Features

- ✅ **Smart Prompt Templates**: Pre-built templates for ChatGPT, Midjourney, DALL-E, Claude, and Stable Diffusion
- ✅ **Prompt Enhancer**: Paste a weak prompt and get an optimized version instantly
- ✅ **Variable Insertion**: Customize prompts with dynamic variables for reusable templates
- ✅ **Collections & Sharing**: Save, organize, and share prompts with the community
- ✅ **Multi-Model Support**: Generate optimized prompts for 10+ AI models

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Select AI Model**: Choose your target AI (ChatGPT, Midjourney, etc.)
2. **Choose Output Type**: Specify desired output (code, image, text, data analysis)
3. **Customize & Generate**: Adjust creativity and detail sliders, then generate your prompt
