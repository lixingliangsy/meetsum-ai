1. How does the points system work?

✅ Status: Implemented

Answer: You earn points by completing workouts and challenges. Base points: 100 points per workout. Duration bonus: 1 point per minute. Calorie bonus: 0.5 points per calorie burned. Points are automatically calculated and added to your profile.

Read detailed explanation

Points Calculation:

  • Base Points: 100 points for each completed workout
  • Duration Bonus: 1 point per minute of workout (e.g., 30-minute workout = 30 bonus points)
  • Calorie Bonus: 0.5 points per calorie burned (e.g., 200 calories = 100 bonus points)
  • Challenge Bonus: Extra points for completing challenges (varies by challenge difficulty)

Example: A 30-minute strength training workout that burns 200 calories would earn: 100 (base) + 30 (duration) + 100 (calories) = 230 points total.

Points are automatically added to your profile after each workout. You can track your points on your profile page and see your rank on the leaderboard.

4. What are daily/weekly/monthly challenges?

✅ Status: Implemented

Answer: Challenges are special tasks that award bonus points. Daily challenges reset every day, weekly challenges reset every week, and monthly challenges reset every month. Completing challenges awards extra points on top of your workout points.

Read detailed explanation

Challenge Types:

  • 📅 Daily Challenges: Reset at midnight. Usually easier (100-200 points).
  • 📆 Weekly Challenges: Reset on Monday. Medium difficulty (300-600 points).
  • 🗓️ Monthly Challenges: Reset on the 1st. Hardest (1000-2000 points).

Difficulty Levels:

  • ● Easy: Simple tasks, lower points
  • ● Medium: Moderate effort required
  • ● Hard: Significant commitment, highest points

Click "完成训练" (Complete Training) on a challenge to mark it as completed and earn the bonus points!

5. How does the level system work?

✅ Status: Implemented

Answer: Your level is calculated as: Floor(Total Points / 1000) + 1. For example, if you have 3750 points, your level is 5. Each level represents your progress and dedication. Higher levels unlock more recognition in the community.

Read detailed explanation

Level Calculation:

Level = Floor(Total Points / 1000) + 1

Examples:
- 0-999 points → Level 1
- 1000-1999 points → Level 2
- 2000-2999 points → Level 3
- 3000-3999 points → Level 4
- 4000-4999 points → Level 5
...
- 9000-9999 points → Level 10 (Fitness Master badge!)

Progress Bar: Your profile shows a progress bar indicating how close you are to the next level. The formula for the next level is: (Current Level) * 1000 points.
