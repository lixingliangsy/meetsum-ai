# 🎮 Fitness Gamification Platform

[![Deploy with Vercel](https://vercel.com/button)](https://vercel.com/new/clone?repository-url=https://github.com/yourusername/fitness-gamification-platform)

> Gamify your fitness routine. Earn points, unlock achievements, and compete with friends.

## ✨ Features

- ✅ **Points & Levels**: Earn XP for every workout
- ✅ **Achievement Badges**: Unlock rewards for consistency
- ✅ **Leaderboards**: Compete with friends and global users
- ✅ **Daily Quests**: Complete challenges to earn bonus rewards
- ✅ **Social Guilds**: Join fitness communities and collaborate

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Create Profile**: Set up your avatar and fitness goals
2. **Start Working Out**: Log workouts to earn XP
3. **Level Up**: Unlock achievements and climb leaderboards
