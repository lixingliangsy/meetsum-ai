The calculator multiplies the total meeting duration by the combined hourly rates of all attendees. For example, a 1-hour meeting with 5 attendees earning $50/hour each costs $250. You can customize salary levels, currency, and overhead multipliers (typically 1.3x for benefits and overhead). - Formula: `(Total Attendee Hourly Rate) × (Meeting Hours) × (Overhead Multiplier)` - Default overhead multiplier: 1.3x (industry standard for benefits, taxes, equipment) - Supported currencies: USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, JPY ---
