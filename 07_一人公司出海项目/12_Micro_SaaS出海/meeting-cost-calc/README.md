# 💰 Meeting Cost Calculator

[![Deploy with Vercel](https://vercel.com/button)](https://vercel.com/new/clone?repository-url=https://github.com/yourusername/meeting-cost-calc)

> Calculate the true cost of your meetings instantly. Track attendee time, salaries, and ROI.

## ✨ Features

- ✅ **Real-Time Cost Tracking**: Watch meeting costs accumulate in real-time
- ✅ **Salary-Based Calculation**: Input attendee roles or hourly rates for accurate costing
- ✅ **Meeting Analytics**: Identify which meetings deliver ROI and which waste time
- ✅ **Calendar Integration**: Connect with Google Calendar for automatic tracking
- ✅ **Team Dashboard**: View historical data and optimize meeting culture

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Add Meeting Details**: Enter attendee names, roles, or hourly rates
2. **Start Timer**: Begin tracking when the meeting starts
3. **Review & Optimize**: Analyze costs and get recommendations to reduce meeting waste
