## Detailed Description (200-300 words)
Social Proof Widget is a lightweight, no-code tool that helps you display real-time social proof on your website to boost conversions. Studies show that 92% of consumers trust recommendations from peers over branded content, and adding social proof to your website can increase conversions by up to 34%.

Our widget lets you display three types of social proof: 1) Customer reviews and ratings, 2) Real-time user activity (e.g., "12 people are viewing this product now"), 3) Trust badges (e.g., "Featured in Forbes", "SSL Secured", "30-Day Money-Back Guarantee"). You can customize the widget’s design to match your website’s branding, and it loads asynchronously so it won’t slow down your site.

It integrates with popular e-commerce platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce), website builders (WordPress, Wix, Squarespace), and marketing tools (Mailchimp, HubSpot). The basic version is free for up to 1000 monthly visitors, and premium plans scale with your traffic. It’s the easiest way to build trust with your website visitors and turn more of them into customers.
