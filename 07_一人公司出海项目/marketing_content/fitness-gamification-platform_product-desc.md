## Features Section Copy (5 Features)
### Feature 1: Earn Points for Every Workout
Log any workout (run, yoga, strength training, etc.) to earn points. The more intense the workout, the more points you get.

### Feature 2: Unlock Achievement Badges
Hit fitness milestones to unlock exclusive badges (e.g., first 5km run, 10-workout streak, 100km total distance).

### Feature 3: Compete with Friends
Join leaderboards to compete with friends and other community members. See who can earn the most points in a month.

### Feature 4: Community Challenges
Join monthly challenges (e.g., step count challenge, workout streak challenge) to stay accountable and win prizes.

### Feature 5: Auto-Sync with Fitness Trackers
Automatically log your workouts from Apple Health, Fitbit, Garmin, and other popular fitness trackers. No manual entry required.

## About Page Copy
### Brand Story
Fitness Gamification Platform was founded by a fitness enthusiast who struggled to stay motivated to work out consistently. After using gamification mechanics to stick to his own routine for 6 months straight, he realized this approach could help millions of people who struggle with the same problem. He assembled a small team of fitness and gaming experts to build an app that makes working out as fun as playing a video game.

### Mission
Our mission is to help people build consistent fitness habits by making working out fun and rewarding. We believe everyone can enjoy fitness, no matter their current fitness level.

### Vision
We envision a world where everyone has the motivation and tools to live a healthy, active life.
