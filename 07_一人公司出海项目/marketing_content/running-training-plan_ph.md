## Detailed Description (200-300 words)
Running Training Plan Generator is a tool that creates custom running training plans for runners of all levels. Whether you’re training for your first 5K, trying to hit a personal best in the marathon, or just want to run consistently for general fitness, our tool generates a plan tailored to your goals, current fitness level, and schedule.

You input your goal (e.g., finish a 5K, run a sub-2-hour half-marathon), your current running ability (e.g., can run 3km without stopping), your weekly availability (e.g., 3 days per week, 30 minutes per session), and any past injuries. The tool then generates a 8-16 week training plan with detailed daily workouts, including easy runs, speed work, long runs, and rest days.

The plans are based on proven running science to help you avoid injuries and improve performance. You can adjust the plan as needed, and track your progress in the app. The basic version is free, and premium users get access to 1:1 coaching from certified running coaches, custom nutrition plans, and injury prevention guides.
