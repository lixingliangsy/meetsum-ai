## Features Section Copy (4 Features)
### Feature 1: Fast Screenshot Capture
Capture full screens, specific windows, or custom regions with a single hotkey. No complicated setup required.

### Feature 2: Powerful Annotation Tools
Add arrows, text boxes, highlights, shapes, and blur to your screenshots. All annotations are editable before saving.

### Feature 3: Cross-Platform Support
Works on Windows, Mac, and Linux. Browser extensions for Chrome and Firefox let you capture web pages directly.

### Feature 4: Instant Sharing
Save screenshots in PNG, JPG, or PDF format, or share them directly to Slack, Jira, Email, and other tools.

## About Page Copy
### Brand Story
Screenshot Annotator was built by a remote team that struggled with miscommunication when giving visual feedback. After wasting countless hours writing long text descriptions that were often misunderstood, the team decided to build a simple, free tool that makes visual communication easy for everyone. What started as an internal tool is now used by thousands of people around the world to communicate more clearly.

### Mission
Our mission is to make visual communication accessible to everyone. We believe that a simple screenshot with annotations is worth 1000 words of text description.

### Vision
We envision a world where all feedback and instructions are clear, visual, and easy to understand, reducing miscommunication and saving time for everyone.
