## Product Name

## Tags (5 tags max)

## Tagline (≤50 words)
Calculate the real cost of your meetings based on attendee count and hourly rates. Stop wasting budget on unproductive meetings.

## Detailed Description (200-300 words)
Meeting Cost Calculator is a simple, powerful tool designed to help businesses of all sizes identify and reduce the hidden costs of unproductive meetings. Did you know that a 1-hour meeting with 10 employees earning $50/hour costs your company $500? Most teams don’t realize how much budget is drained by unnecessary or poorly run meetings.

Our tool lets you input attendee roles, hourly rates, and meeting duration to get an accurate total cost breakdown. You can also track recurring meetings to see long-term cost impacts. Use the insights to cut unnecessary invites, shorten meeting times, and prioritize only high-value discussions.

It’s free to use, requires no sign-up, and works on all devices. For teams that want advanced features like team cost tracking and meeting ROI analysis, we offer a low-cost premium plan. Start saving your company’s time and money today.
