# Fitness Gamification Platform - Product Hunt Launch Copy

## Product Name
Fitness Gamification Platform

## Tags (5 tags max)
Fitness, Health & Wellness, Gamification, SaaS, Habit Building

## Tagline (≤50 words)
Turn your fitness routine into a fun game. Earn points, unlock badges, and compete with friends to stay motivated.

## Detailed Description (200-300 words)
Fitness Gamification Platform is a mobile and web app that makes working out fun and addictive. We all know we should exercise regularly, but staying motivated is hard — that’s where gamification comes in. Our app lets you log your workouts, earn points for every activity, unlock achievement badges, and compete with friends on leaderboards.

You can set custom fitness goals (e.g., run 10km per week, do 3 strength training sessions per week) and track your progress. The more you work out, the more points and badges you earn. You can also join community challenges (e.g., step count challenges, workout streak challenges) to stay accountable.

It integrates with popular fitness trackers like Apple Health, Fitbit, and Garmin to automatically log your activities. The basic version is free, and premium users get access to custom challenges, 1:1 coaching, and exclusive badges. Whether you’re a fitness newbie or a seasoned athlete, our app will help you stay motivated and build a consistent fitness habit.
