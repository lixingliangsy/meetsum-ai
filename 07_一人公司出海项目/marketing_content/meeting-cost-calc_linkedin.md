# Meeting Cost Calculator - LinkedIn Promotion Article

## Article Title
How Much Did Your Last Meeting Cost Your Company?

## Article Body (500-800 words)
Let’s do a quick exercise: think about the last 1-hour meeting you attended. How many people were in the room? What’s their average hourly rate? If you had 8 attendees with an average rate of $40/hour, that meeting cost your company $320. Now multiply that by the 23 meetings the average employee attends per week (Atlassian data) — that’s over $7,300 per week, or $380,000 per year for a 50-person team. Shocking, right?

Most businesses track marketing spend, software costs, and office rent in detail — but very few track the cost of their meetings. That’s a huge missed opportunity, because meetings are one of the biggest hidden costs for most companies. Unproductive meetings don’t just waste time — they waste real budget that could be spent on growth, R&D, or employee bonuses.

That’s exactly why we built Meeting Cost Calculator. It’s a free, no-signup tool that calculates the exact cost of any meeting in seconds. Just enter the number of attendees, their average hourly rate, and the meeting duration, and you’ll get a detailed cost breakdown. You can also track recurring meetings to see their long-term cost impact on your budget.

Who is this tool for? It’s for team leads who want to cut unnecessary meeting costs, operations managers who need to optimize team budgets, and executives who want to improve company-wide productivity. Use it before sending a meeting invite to check if the discussion is worth the cost. Use it after a meeting to track how much budget was spent. Use it to convince your team to cut unnecessary attendees or shorten meeting times.

The value add is clear: our tool helps you identify unproductive meetings and cut them, reduce attendee count for low-value meetings, prioritize only high-value discussions, and save thousands of dollars in wasted meeting costs every year. For example, one of our early users cut their team’s weekly meeting costs by 42% in the first month of using the tool, simply by removing unnecessary attendees from 3 recurring meetings.

Best of all, the core tool is completely free to use, no account required. We also offer a premium plan for teams that want advanced features like team-wide cost tracking, meeting ROI analysis, and custom reporting.
