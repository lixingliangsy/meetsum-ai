## Detailed Description (200-300 words)
Screenshot Annotator is a lightweight, easy-to-use tool for anyone who needs to share visual feedback or instructions. How many times have you spent 10 minutes writing a long text description of a UI bug, only for the recipient to still not understand what you mean? With Screenshot Annotator, you can take a screenshot, add arrows, text boxes, highlights, and shapes in seconds, and share it instantly.

It works on Windows, Mac, and Linux, and has a browser extension for Chrome and Firefox. You can capture full screens, specific windows, or custom regions. All annotations are editable, so you can adjust them before saving or sharing. You can also blur sensitive information in screenshots with one click.

The basic version is free, with premium features like cloud storage, team collaboration, and custom annotation templates. It’s perfect for designers giving feedback, developers reporting bugs, managers giving instructions, and anyone who wants to communicate more clearly with visuals.
