## Features Section Copy (4 Features)
### Feature 1: 100% Personalized Plans
Input your goal, current fitness, schedule, and past injuries to get a training plan tailored to you.

### Feature 2: Science-Backed Workouts
All plans are designed by certified running coaches, with a mix of easy runs, speed work, long runs, and rest days to prevent injuries.

### Feature 3: Progress Tracking
Log your workouts, track your mileage, and see your pace improve over time in the app.

### Feature 4: Adjustable Plans
Modify your plan if your schedule changes or you need to take extra rest days. The tool will adjust the remaining workouts automatically.

## About Page Copy
### Brand Story
Running Training Plan Generator was founded by a runner who got injured twice following generic training plans. After researching running science and creating a personalized plan that helped him finish his first marathon without injury, he decided to build a tool to help other runners do the same. Our team of runners and coaches is dedicated to helping people hit their running goals safely and effectively.

### Mission
Our mission is to make running accessible and safe for everyone. We believe that everyone can enjoy running and hit their goals with the right training plan.

### Vision
We envision a world where no runner gets injured from a poorly designed training plan, and everyone can experience the joy of crossing the finish line of their target race.
