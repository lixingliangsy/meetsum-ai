Social Proof Widget was founded by an e-commerce entrepreneur who struggled to increase his store’s conversion rate. After adding social proof to his product pages and seeing a 40% lift in conversions, he realized that every website could benefit from this simple tactic. He built a tool to make adding social proof easy and affordable for small businesses, and now thousands of businesses use it to boost their conversions.
