## Features Section Copy (4 Features)
### Feature 1: Instant Cost Calculation
Enter attendee count, average hourly rate, and meeting duration to get an accurate total cost breakdown in seconds. No complex setup required.

### Feature 2: Recurring Meeting Tracking
Track costs for weekly or monthly recurring meetings to see long-term budget impacts and identify opportunities to save.

### Feature 3: No Signup Required
Use the core tool for free immediately, no account creation or payment required. Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

### Feature 4: Cost Comparison Tool
Compare the costs of different meeting setups (e.g., 10 attendees vs. 5 attendees) to find the most cost-effective option for your team.

## About Page Copy
### Brand Story
Meeting Cost Calculator was built by a solo founder who saw too many teams wasting time and money on unnecessary meetings. After calculating his own former company’s meeting costs and realizing they were spending over $100k per year on unproductive meetings, he decided to build a tool to help other teams do the same. What started as a side project to solve his own pain point has now helped hundreds of teams reduce their meeting costs and improve productivity.

### Mission
Our mission is to help businesses of all sizes run more efficient meetings, save budget, and focus on what really matters. We believe every meeting should have a clear purpose and a positive ROI for the company.

### Vision
We envision a world where unnecessary meetings are a thing of the past, and every meeting brings real value to the team and the business.
