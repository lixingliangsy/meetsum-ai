# Async Standup Tool

Replace time-wasting daily meetings with quick async check-ins. Team members post updates on their schedule, and you get a clean summary every morning.

- Clean, intuitive interface
- Fast, reliable performance
- Works on any device

- Anyone who values simplicity and speed
